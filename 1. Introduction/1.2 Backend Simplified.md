# BACKEND TLDR LOL

Every website can be split into 2 parts which are frontend and backend. Frontend is what user see on their screen all that visual etc and backend is what saves and manages your data. For example if you are on Lazada or Shopee, the backend would store your order history, your profile, search result etc.

Say you are on Shopee right now and if you click on order. What actually happens? So basically every computer that is connected to internet can send message across the internet to another computer, that is also connected to the internet. So to visualise this, Shopee have their computer somewhere in the world and our computer is going to send a message continuing our order to that shopee. In this scenario, our computer (the one sending message) is called the *client* and the Shopee computer receiving the message is the *server*.
